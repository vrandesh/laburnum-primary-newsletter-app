Hi everyone, I’ve put together a small Laburnum Reader App to make the school newsletters easier to read and browse in one place.

To be honest, the current way of going through newsletters can feel a bit old-school and clunky, especially when we’re trying to quickly find updates, dates, notices, or older information. In today’s world, parents need something more readable, searchable, and mobile-friendly.

The idea is to make the newsletters feel cleaner, more organised, and more current-gen — almost like a simple newspaper-style reader where updates are easier to scan and read.

Search is also much easier now, so parents can quickly look up key information instead of scrolling through long newsletter pages.

It’s still an early version, but I thought it could be useful as a parent-led feature to quickly access school updates and past newsletters in a simpler way.

Happy to take feedback, suggestions, or ideas on how this can be improved further.
